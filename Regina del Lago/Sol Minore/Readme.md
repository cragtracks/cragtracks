## Sol Minore

Type: Multipitch route, sport climbing

Number of pitches: 3

Total length: 90m

Protection: bolted

Grades: 4b-5c and a piece of 6a

Access: Easy, 10 min

Parking: available

Location: [45.86342434, 10.82668751](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?mlat=45.86342434&mlon=10.82668751#map=19/45.86342/10.82669)
